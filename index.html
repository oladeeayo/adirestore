Our Story

Born From Indigo,
Built With Purpose

The Adire Store was founded to preserve and celebrate the ancient Yorùbá art of resist-dyeing. Every garment carries centuries of cultural heritage — from the cassava paste (eleko) resist patterns to the natural indigo dye that gives each piece its signature deep blue.

We work directly with artisan communities in Abeokuta and Ibadan, ensuring fair wages and keeping these traditional techniques alive for future generations.
